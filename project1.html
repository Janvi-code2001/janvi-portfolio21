Virtual Grocery Assistant App

Role

UX/UI Designer

Tools Used

Figma, Google Forms

Duration

2 months

Project Description

Our team designed a Virtual Grocery Shopping Assistant aimed at helping students and busy professionals streamline their online grocery experience. The goal was to create a mobile platform that delivers personalized shopping lists, one-tap reordering, real-time stock updates, and eco-friendly product suggestions.

Problem

Online grocery shoppers often face a time-consuming and frustrating experience due to manual searches, lack of personalized recommendations, and poor delivery reliability. These issues are especially challenging for users balancing busy schedules or working on tight budgets. Our research revealed common pain points including difficulty finding relevant products, price concerns, and last-minute product cancellations. The need was clear: a solution that delivers speed, personalization, and smart planning—while staying accessible and user-friendly.

1. User Interviews

  • Conducted in-depth interviews with students and working professionals to understand their online grocery challenges.
  • Identified pain points like slow product search, poor price comparison, and unreliable delivery.
  • Helped define features like one-tap reordering and real-time stock updates.

2. User Research

  • Conducted surveys and empathy mapping with students and professionals who shop online for groceries.
  • Identified issues such as lack of personalization, search fatigue, and poor delivery experience.
  • These insights helped prioritize features like smart lists, express checkout, and delivery slot tracking.

3. Competitive Analysis

  • Researched grocery apps like Instacart, Walmart, and Amazon Fresh.
  • Identified missing features such as AI-driven personalization and smart reordering.
  • Used insights to define core features of our assistant like budget tools and eco-friendly alerts.

4. Persona Development

  • Created personas for users like students and working professionals with diverse grocery shopping habits.
  • Mapped user goals (speed, savings), frustrations (cancellations, no suggestions), and behaviors (mobile usage, app trust).
  • These personas influenced design features like reminders, smart lists, and streamlined UI.

5. Visual Design

  • Developed a modern and clean design system using a teal-accent color palette.
  • Used Roboto for headings and Shippori Mincho for readable body text.
  • Defined consistent UI elements for accessibility and user trust.

6. Prototype

  • Developed high-fidelity prototypes in Figma based on core user needs.
  • Included smart grocery list generation, real-time stock status, and eco-friendly filters.
  • Designed an intuitive mobile-first UI with simplified navigation and checkout flow.
